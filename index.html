The Challenge

Given the nature of the randomly generated map, the real challenge is in getting a well-trained AI that is competent in multi-objective optimization. In particular the AI has to consider between, collecting coins, killing opponents, taking hits, staying still etc. Our goal is to make an AI that considers all these requirements and comes up with the best strategy possible!

Game Rules

  • Game environment is made out of brick walls, stone walls, water and blank cells.
  • Tanks are capable of moving, rotating and shooting bullets
  • Coins will appear on the map due to random treasures and spoils of war.
  • When a player collect a pile of coils, his own coin count and the point count will get increased by that amount.
  • Breaking a brick wall from a bullet will add points to the player.
  • A player acquiring a life pack will increase a 20% of the initial health of that player
  • Bullet hit will reduce the a 10% of the initial health of that player.
  • A player who has 0% of health will die and drop his coins at the point of death.
  • Death player won't lose current points.
  • 25% of the victim will be added to the killer.
  • Dropped pile of coin is same as the randomly created pile of coin.
  • Hit on an obstacle (Brick wall or a Stone wall) will reduce marks.
  • Players can shoot over water.
  • If a player moves into water, player will be killed.
  • One of the reason from all the players are dead, the game life time counter expires, all the coin piles finished appearing on the map and all the appeared coin piles either has been claimed by a player or disappeared will course the end of the game.
  • At the end of the game, the player with most points will be declared the winner.
